Europe Experiences

Historic cities, art, and countryside adventures.

Unforgettable Experiences in Europe by Region

  • Southern Europe: Savoring tapas in Barcelona (Spain), gondola rides in Venice (Italy), and exploring the Acropolis in Athens (Greece).
  • Western Europe: Climbing the Eiffel Tower in Paris (France), cycling through Amsterdam’s canals (Netherlands), and enjoying Belgian chocolate in Brussels.
  • Eastern Europe: Touring Prague Castle (Czech Republic), relaxing in Budapest’s thermal baths (Hungary), and discovering Kraków’s Old Town (Poland).
  • Northern Europe: Cruising Norway’s fjords, chasing the Northern Lights in Swedish Lapland, and exploring Viking history in Copenhagen (Denmark).
  • Central Europe: Hiking in the Swiss Alps (Switzerland), wandering Vienna’s imperial palaces (Austria), and tasting Bavarian pretzels at Munich’s Oktoberfest (Germany).

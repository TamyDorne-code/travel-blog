Antarctica

Ice, wildlife, and expedition adventures.

Top Places to Visit in Antarctica

South Pole

The southernmost point on Earth, reached by special expeditions.

Deception Island

Volcanic island with hot springs and penguin colonies.

Paradise Bay

Stunning glaciers, icebergs and breathtaking scenery.

South Shetland Islands

Home to diverse wildlife and stunning landscapes.

Antarctic Peninsula

Rich in wildlife, glaciers, and natural harbours.

South Georgia

Known for its wildlife, including seals and penguins.

Falkland Islands

Remote islands with unique wildlife and rich history.

Lemaire Channel

Scenic waterway known for its dramatic cliffs and icebergs.

Port Lockroy

Historic British research station and museum.

Ross Sea

Large colony of peguins, majestic glaciers, historical sites, icebergs and immense natural beauty.

King George Island

Largest island in the South Shetland Islands, known for its research stations.

Colossal Penguin Colony

One of the largest penguin colonies in the world, located on Snow Hill Island.

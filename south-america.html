South America

Rainforests, mountains, and ancient ruins.

Top Places to Visit in South America

Machu Picchu, Peru

Ancient Incan city high in the Andes mountains.

Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Famous beaches, Christ the Redeemer, and Carnival.

Patagonia, Argentina & Chile

Stunning glaciers, mountains, and wild landscapes.

Cartagena, Colombia

A gorgeous fishing village, exellent beaches and a historic old town.

Quito, Ecuador

Historic city with colonial architecture and stunning views.

La Paz, Bolivia

Exciting and unique city. Streets here climb through neighborhoods so steep that the easiest way to get around is by cable car, used by everyone from tourists to commuters.

Mendoza, Argentina

Famous for its wine, especially Malbec.

Easter Island, Chile

Remote island with mysterious Moai statues.

Santiago, Chile

Vibrant capital city with a mix of modern and historic.

Bogota, Colombia

Delicious food , fine eating, budding cultural scene and gorgeous landscapes.

Iguazu Falls, Argentina & Brazil

One of the largest and most impressive waterfalls in the world.

Montevideo

City full of charms, where history meets tranquility. It's scenic beaches and lively festivals create an atmosphere that's both relaxing and breathtaking.

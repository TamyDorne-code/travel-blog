South America Experiences

Amazon, Andes, and ancient civilization tales.

Unforgettable Experiences in South America by Region

  • Andes Mountains: Hiking the Inca Trail to Machu Picchu (Peru), exploring the salt flats of Uyuni (Bolivia), and climbing Cotopaxi volcano (Ecuador).
  • Amazon Basin: Spotting wildlife on a river cruise (Brazil, Peru), visiting indigenous communities, and exploring the rainforest canopy.
  • Patagonia: Trekking in Torres del Paine (Chile), viewing glaciers in Los Glaciares National Park (Argentina), and wildlife watching in Tierra del Fuego.
  • Atlantic Coast: Dancing at Carnival in Rio de Janeiro (Brazil), relaxing on the beaches of Florianópolis (Brazil), and exploring colonial Salvador.
  • Northern South America: Visiting Angel Falls (Venezuela), exploring Cartagena’s walled city (Colombia), and hiking in the Andes of Colombia.
  • Southern Cone: Tasting wine in Mendoza (Argentina), enjoying the culture of Buenos Aires, and exploring the Lake District of Chile and Argentina.

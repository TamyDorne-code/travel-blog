Africa Travel Tips

Packing, safety, and local advice for Africa.

Top Tips for Traveling in Africa

Clothes

Pack light, breathable clothing for the climate. Neutral colors are best for safaris. Bring a hat and a light jacket for cooler evenings.

Water

Always carry bottled water and stay hydrated, especially in hot regions. Avoid drinking tap water unless it's confirmed safe.

Culture

Respect local customs and traditions. Dress modestly in rural areas and always ask before taking photos of people.

Health

Get necessary vaccinations before your trip. Carry a basic first aid kit and any prescription medications you need.

Wildlife

Be aware of wildlife safety rules on safari. Always listen to your guide and never approach wild animals.

Money

Carry cash in small denominations for local markets and tips. Credit cards may not be accepted everywhere.

Protection

Use sunscreen and insect repellent daily to protect against sunburn and mosquito bites.

Language

Learn a few phrases in the local language for a better experience and to connect with locals.

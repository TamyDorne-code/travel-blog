Antarctica Experiences

Ice, wildlife, and expedition adventures.

Unforgettable Experiences in Antarctica by Place

  • Antarctic Peninsula: Kayaking among icebergs, hiking on glaciers, and spotting penguin colonies along the dramatic coastline.
  • South Shetland Islands: Visiting Deception Island’s volcanic caldera, observing seals on Livingston Island, and exploring research bases.
  • Ross Sea: Cruising past massive ice shelves, seeing historic huts from early explorers, and encountering emperor penguins.
  • Weddell Sea: Searching for tabular icebergs, watching for whales, and learning about Ernest Shackleton’s legendary expedition.
  • King George Island: Touring international research stations and experiencing the unique community at the gateway to the continent.

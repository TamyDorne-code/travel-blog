Top Tips for Traveling in South America

Clothes

Language

Spanish and Portuguese are widely spoken. Learn basic phrases or use a translation app for easier communication.

Money

Carry cash in small bills. ATMs are common in cities, but less so in rural areas. Credit cards may not be accepted everywhere.

Transport

Long-distance buses are popular and affordable. Domestic flights save time for large distances.

Health

Check vaccination requirements. Use insect repellent and drink bottled or filtered water to avoid illness.

Safety

Be aware of your surroundings, especially in crowded places. Use official taxis and avoid displaying valuables.

Nature

Respect wildlife and natural sites. Book tours with reputable operators for activities like trekking or Amazon visits.

Food

Try local dishes and fresh fruits. Eat at busy places for better food safety.

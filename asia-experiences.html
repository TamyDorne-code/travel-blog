Asia Experiences

Temples, street food, and festival tales from Asia.

Unforgettable Experiences in Asia

East Asia

  • Cherry Blossom Season (Japan): Picnicking under blooming sakura trees in Tokyo.
  • Great Wall Hike (China): Walking along ancient stones with breathtaking mountain views.
  • Temple Stay (South Korea): Experience Buddhist monastic life and meditation.
  • Night Markets (Taiwan): Savor street food and shop in vibrant night markets.

Southeast Asia

  • Exploring Angkor Wat (Cambodia): Sunrise at the world’s largest temple complex.
  • Street Food Tour (Thailand): Sampling pad thai, mango sticky rice, and spicy curries in Bangkok.
  • Floating Markets (Vietnam): Shopping and eating from boats on the Mekong Delta.
  • Island Hopping (Philippines): Discovering white sand beaches and turquoise waters.

South Asia

  • Taj Mahal Visit (India): Marvel at the iconic symbol of love at sunrise.
  • Tea Plantations (Sri Lanka): Tour lush green hills and taste fresh Ceylon tea.
  • Everest Base Camp Trek (Nepal): Hike through the Himalayas to the foot of the world’s highest peak.
  • Traditional Festivals (Bangladesh): Experience colorful celebrations like Pohela Boishakh.

Central Asia

  • Silk Road Adventure (Uzbekistan): Explore ancient cities like Samarkand and Bukhara.
  • Yurt Stay (Kyrgyzstan): Sleep in a traditional yurt on the shores of Issyk-Kul Lake.
  • Almaty Mountains (Kazakhstan): Hike and ski in the scenic Tien Shan range.

Western Asia (Middle East)

  • Petra by Night (Jordan): Walk through the Siq to the Treasury lit by thousands of candles.
  • Grand Bazaar (Turkey): Shop for spices, carpets, and crafts in Istanbul’s historic market.
  • Desert Safari (UAE): Ride camels and watch the sunset over golden dunes.

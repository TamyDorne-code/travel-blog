Africa

Safari adventures, pyramids, and vibrant cultures.

Top Places to Visit in Africa

Serengeti National Park, Tanzania

Witness the Great Migration and Africa's iconic wildlife.

Cape Town, South Africa

Table Mountain, beaches, and vibrant city life.

Pyramids of Giza, Egypt

Marvel at ancient wonders and desert landscapes.

Murchison Falls National Park, Uganda

Majestic waterfalls, diverse wildlife and the chance to spot the rare shoebill stork.

Stone Town, Zanzibar

Blending African, Indian, Arabian and European cultures.

Okavango Delta, Botswana

A unique inland delta with rich wildlife and stunning landscapes.

Mauritius Island

Pristine beaches, coral reefs, and lush landscapes.

Marrakesh City, Morocco

History, Architecture, traditional culture and, of course, delicious food.

Sossusvlei Desert, Namibia

Stunning red dunes and surreal landscapes.

Ngorongoro Crater, Tanzania

A UNESCO World Heritage site with diverse wildlife.

Reunion Island

Active volcano and several mountains.

Djenne Mosque, Mali

World's largest mud-brick structure and a UNESCO World Heritage site.

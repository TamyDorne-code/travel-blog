Australia Travel Tips

Outback, wildlife, and weather tips.

Top Tips for Traveling in Australia

Clothes

Pack light, breathable clothing for hot days and a jacket for cool nights. Bring swimwear and sturdy shoes for hiking.

Sun Safety

Use sunscreen, wear a hat, and sunglasses—the Australian sun is very strong.

Beaches

Swim only at patrolled beaches and always between the red and yellow flags for safety.

Wildlife

Be cautious of wildlife, especially in the outback. Never feed wild animals and watch for snakes and spiders.

Distances

Australia is vast—plan your travel routes carefully and allow extra time for long drives.

Food

Try local foods like Tim Tams, Vegemite, and fresh seafood. Tap water is safe to drink in most places.

Culture

Respect Aboriginal culture and heritage sites. Learn about local customs and ask before taking photos.

Connectivity

Mobile coverage can be limited in remote areas. Download maps and info before heading outback.

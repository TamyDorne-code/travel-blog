Sydney

Adelaide

Gold Coast

Surfing beaches, theme parks and carnivals.

Cairns

Gateway to the Great Barrier Reef and tropical rainforests.

Christchurch

Garden city with parks, museums, and a vibrant arts scene.

Wellington

Capital city with a vibrant arts scene and beautiful harbor.

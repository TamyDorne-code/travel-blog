Europe

Historic cities, art, and beautiful countryside.

Top Places to Visit in Europe

Paris, France

The Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and charming cafes.

Rome, Italy

Ancient ruins, the Colosseum, and delicious Italian cuisine.

Santorini, Greece

Whitewashed houses, blue domes, and stunning sunsets.

York, England

Gorgeous park, the Museum Gardens loaded with plants and flowers to wander about. The shambles connect you to the real life Harry Potter scene.

Granada, Spain

A stunning Moorish palace that is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Dublin, Ireland

History, positive energy and amazing people.

Ljubljana, Slovena

Very livable, lively city and fresh food from local markets.

Edinburgh, Scotland

The city's distinct character, gothic architecture and rich cultural heritage.

Strasbourg, France

Ruins and wine are two hallmarks of Strasbourg. It has the biggest and best christmas market.

Belfast, Northern Ireland

Belfast was the birthplace of the RMS Titanic, the world' most famous ship which, when it was constructed in the early 1900s, was longer than the height of the world's tallest building at 882 feet and six inches in length.

Istanbul, Turkey

Unique position as a bridge between continents.

Alba Iluia, Romania

Christian country, white truffle and wine production.
